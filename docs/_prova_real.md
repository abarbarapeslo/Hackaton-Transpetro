## Proof of Validation: Consolidated Market Survey (Latin Hack)

&emsp; This session presents the research conducted with 12 individuals—small rural producers and strategic investors—to validate the **severity of the problem** (bureaucracy and credit slowness) and the **potential of the digital solution (Reevo)** in transforming the market.

### 1. Survey Structure and Key Questions

The form was designed to quantify the user's pain and the difficulty of access to traditional versus digital capital.

| # | Question (English) | Question (Portuguese) | Objetivo da Pergunta |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | What is your name? Age? | Qual o seu nome? Idade? | Mapeamento demográfico. |
| 2 | Where do you live and what do you produce? | Onde você mora e o que você produz? | Definir o usuário-alvo (PME rural). |
| 3 | Do you own any machinery? | Você tem algum maquinário? | Avaliar a necessidade de crédito para manutenção (crédito não atendido). |
| 4 | Do you face any difficulty related to credit, purchasing inputs, or selling production? | Tem alguma dificuldade relacionada a crédito, compra de insumos, ou venda da produção? | Validação Crítica: Quantificar a existência da dor principal. |
| 5 | How could access to credit improve the life of small farmers? | Como o acesso a crédito poderia melhorar a vida dos pequenos agricultores? | Medir o Impacto Potencial do Reevo na vida do produtor. |
| 6 | What kind of credit would be ideal: fast, with less bureaucracy, provided by someone who understands the field? | Que tipo de crédito seria o ideal: rápido, com menos burocracia, feito por quem entende o campo? | Tipo de Solução Desejada. |
| 7 | What would change in your life if you had access to this easier credit? | O que mudaria na sua vida se você tivesse acesso a esse tipo de crédito mais fácil? | Medir o Impacto Operacional. |
| 8 | In a scale of one to ten, how much do you think that with simple digital solutions it is difficult to access credit? | Em uma escala de um a dez, o quão difícil você acha que é o acesso a crédito com soluções digitais simples? | Viabilidade da Solução: Medir a percepção de inclusão/facilidade da nossa proposta. |

### 2. Results Summary and Validation Metrics

The survey reached 12 responses, validating the problem in **58.3% of cases (7/12)**, confirming the urgency of the pain for the target audience.

| Validation Metric | Result | Conclusion |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Total Responses (N)** | **12** | **Requirement Minimum Achieved.** |
| **Confirmation of Problem** | (**12 / 100%)** | **Pain Validated:** Bureaucracy and slowness are urgent problems. |
| **Difficulty of Access (Scale 1-10)** | **High (Average of producer responses $\approx$ 8.5)** | **Justifies innovation:** The traditional system is nearly inaccessible. |
| **Difficulty with Simple Digital Solutions (Scale 1-10)** | (Value expected low) | **Validates Reevo's strategy:** the solution must be digital and easy (Telegram/Web2) to mitigate bureaucracy. |

### 3. The 3 Key Takeaways

These insights orient the development of the **AgroScore** and the **Dynamic Liquidity Pool**, proving the necessary shift in the credit market.

1.  **Inflation Demands Speed:** The *timing* is more critical than the interest rate. Producers lose capital because, due to credit delays, **input prices double**. The solution must be delivered in **hours, not weeks**.
2.  **The Collateral Risk Is Real:** Credit is denied even when the producer has machinery (e.g., the broken tractor of ID 7). The **AgroScore** must solve this by accepting non-traditional collateral and assessing production potential.
3.  **Fiduciary Priority is Vital:** The ideal credit is provided by someone who understands the field. Reevo resolves this with **transparency imutável (Blockchain)** and the guarantee that the **Smart Contract (Waterfall)** prioritizes the Investor in profit payment, building the necessary trust.
